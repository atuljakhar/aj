Dr. Atul Jakhar

National Postdoctoral Fellow
IIT Madras • Chennai, India

My work sits at the intersection of classical mechanics and modern technology, specifically exploring Computational Fluid Mechanics (CFD) and the application of Artificial Intelligence in fluid dynamics.


I investigate the dynamic behavior of fluids through numerical methods and simulations, with a particular focus on instability and chaos. Beyond academia, I am a passionate athlete and motivational speaker, believing that the discipline required in sports translates directly to scientific rigor.
