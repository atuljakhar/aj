About Me

My work sits at the intersection of classical mechanics and modern technology, specifically exploring Computational Fluid Mechanics (CFD) and the application of Artificial Intelligence in fluid dynamics.

I investigate the dynamic behavior of fluids through numerical methods and simulations, with a particular focus on instability and chaos. Passions... discipline required in sports translates directly to scientific rigor.
